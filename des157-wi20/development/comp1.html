Composition 1

For this design composition, I wanted to make the user interface very pleasing and aesthetic as the main focus is to get the user's attention. The focus will be on the transitions and animations while allowing the user to interact with the website. This whole experience will give them a better understanding of innate sexism in the tech industry and how this impacts the interaction with virtual assistants through the role of personas. The colors and visual appeal was to keep it minimalistic since I didn't want to overwhelm the user by combining strong colors with the use of transitions
